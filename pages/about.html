Our Story

Beauty that feels warm, modern, and empowering.

Founded in 2020 by Ruth Kamamia, Blessing Enterprise started with a simple vision: to make high-quality cosmetics accessible to every woman in Kenya. What began as a small WhatsApp business has grown into a trusted beauty destination serving hundreds of happy customers across the country.

We believe that beauty isn't about perfection — it's about feeling confident, authentic, and empowered in your own skin. Every product in our collection is carefully curated to meet our strict quality standards while remaining affordable and accessible.

Our Mission

To make self-care more elegant and accessible through quality cosmetic choices that help women and everyday shoppers feel radiant, confident, and beautiful in their own unique way.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's most trusted boutique where thoughtful beauty products, exceptional service, and personal confidence meet in one welcoming place.

Our Core Values

  • Quality First: We only stock products that meet our strict quality standards
  • Customer-Centric: Your satisfaction is our top priority
  • Authenticity: 100% genuine products, no counterfeits
  • Empowerment: Helping women feel confident and beautiful
  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly packaging and responsible sourcing
